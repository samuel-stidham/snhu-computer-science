**Course Description:**
This foundational course introduces students to the mindsets, traits, and skills necessary for lifelong learning and personal growth. It examines how adaptability supports perseverance in digital and contemporary communities, and how personal values influence responsible decision-making.

---

## Key Topics & Concepts

This course focuses on developing foundational professional and academic competencies:

* **Digital Citizenship & Community:**
    * **Digital Footprint Analysis:** Understanding the impact of one's presence online.
    * **Supportive Digital Communities:** Explaining the traits that foster positive and supportive online engagement.
* **Growth Mindset & Adaptability:**
    * **Growth Mindset Principles:** Identifying and embracing the mindsets and traits that foster personal development.
    * **Adaptability and Perseverance:** Describing how flexibility and adaptability support success when facing change and challenges.
    * **The Role of Feedback:** Analyzing how feedback contributes to the continuous process of learning and refinement.
* **Values and Decision Making:**
    * **Perspectives and Personal Values:** Discussing how diverse viewpoints and individual values guide responsible decision-making.
    * **Goal-Mapping:** Developing practical strategies for setting and achieving goals in the face of change.
